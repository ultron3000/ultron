Data Deletion Request for Cloud TV App

To comply with Google Play Store policies, we provide a clear method for users to request the deletion of their associated account data.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email to our support team with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Required Information:

  • Your Account Email Address (used for the app)
  • Your User Name (if applicable)
  • Confirmation that you wish to delete ALL associated data.

Request Data Deletion via Email

We will process your request and confirm data deletion within 7 business days.
